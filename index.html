Prevention

How to reduce malaria risk

🛏️

Sleep Under Treated Nets

Use insecticide-treated mosquito nets every night to reduce bites while sleeping.

💧

Remove Stagnant Water

Empty containers and drain standing water where mosquitoes can breed.

🧴

Use Repellents

Apply mosquito repellent and protect exposed skin, especially in the evening.

🏥

Test Early

Seek medical testing quickly if fever, chills, or weakness begin suddenly.
